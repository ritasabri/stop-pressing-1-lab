A comprehension-gate research instrument

Your agent wants to do something. Do you know what?

Six approval requests. Three do exactly what the agent says. Three quietly do more.

Your job is the one every agent framework assumes a human does: verify before you approve. Pick an oversight condition — each one gates the approve action differently. This demo is the instrument for a study comparing them.

ConditionsSP1 · between-subjects

Everything here is scripted — no live model, no API, no network calls. Logging is local to your browser and exportable as CSV. Built for the Stop Pressing 1 research project on human oversight of agentic systems.
